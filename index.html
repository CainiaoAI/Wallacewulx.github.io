About me

I am currently the head of intelligent terminal logistics and warehousing logistics in Cainiao Network. I have led several important topics, including urban intelligent logistics network planning, courier scheduling, order distribution and parcel timeliness management. My current research interests include spatiotemporal AI, offline computing advertising, urban computing, and online decision-making.
